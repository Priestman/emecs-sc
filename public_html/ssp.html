Student and Schools Partnership Meeting

SSP meeting schedule (.pdf)

Student Declaration (.pdf)

The SSP Meeting participants are defined as high school aged to undergraduate or roughly 15 – 20 years of age(under 20 year old on the day of Conference start).

The work of the SSP Meeting in frame of the Conference will be organized in forms of the panel discussions and posters session. In the work of the SSP Meeting panel discussions, the world level scientists and educators will participate. One of the SSP Meeting results will be a SSP declaration with a young point of view that is parallel to the main Conference declaration. The SSP Meeting participants will participate in unique SSP Meeting activities which be hosted by Russian student.
