Description of visa services

Return to the personal account

Visa service and support consists of three parts:
1. Visa service of International Department of RSHU (10 euro);
2. Payment for migration service (15 euro);
3. Payment for sending the original visa invitation letter (50 euro).

1. Visa service of International Department of RSHU
Should be payed by each person who need visa.

2. Payment for migration service
Should be payed by each person who need visa, except for citizens of the following countries:

3. Payment for sending the original visa invitation letter
To obtain a Russian visa you must submit the documents, including the original invitation letter, to the Embassy or Consulate of the Russian Federation, which you indicated in the registration form. This original invitation letter we send you by an insured express mail, which costs 50 euros.
However, if the Russian visa is requested by several people from one organization (or one city/country), we can send ONE mail to ONE address with NUMBER of invitations.
In this case, you must do the following procedure:
- ONE person ("visa invitation recipient (VIR)") indicates in point "Sending the original visa invitation letter" as "Send me", that he needs the original invitation letter by the insured express mailand is willing to pay 50 euros; - his name appears in the Visa support list section marked“leader of mail visa invitation”;
- the participant after filling out the page with the visa information, indicate in point "Sending the original visa invitation letter" as “I'll join another user” and choose the "VIR" name from the list in the visa support list section;
- payment for sending the original visa invitation letter will be 0 euro, and the original invitation letter to him will come in same mail that for "VIR".
Please note that the division of the payment for sending the original visa invitation letter (50 Euro) for number of people is IMPOSSIBLE!
