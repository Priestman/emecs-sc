Registration

“Early bird” registration fee (payment before April 14, 2016) is:

• 250 euro for ordinary participants;
• 150 euro for young participants (under 30 year old on the day of Conference start);
• 100 euro for accompanying participants;
• 0 euro for participants of SSP Meeting (under 20 year old on the day of Conference start).

Regular registration fee (payment after April 14, 2016) is:

• 350 euro for ordinary participants;
• 200 euro for young participants (under 30 year old on the day of Conference start);
• 150 euro for accompanying participants;
• 0 euro for participants of SSP Meeting (under 20 year old on the day of Conference start).

• All additional fee components (extra additional papers, visa support) are added to the registration fee.

The Registration fee for ordinary participants includes:

• Complete set of the Conference materials;
• Publication of 1 paper as the main author in the Conference Full-size Proceedings, which are indexed in Scopus and WebOfScience databases;
• Welcome reception;
• Conference Gala-dinner;
• Lunches and coffee-breaks during the Conference;
• City Tour;
• Technical field trip.
Publication of each additional paper in the conference Full-size Proceedings as the main author is possible for 75 euro as an additionalfee component.
