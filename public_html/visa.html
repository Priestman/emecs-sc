VISA

Organizing Committee will prepare the official visa invitations by default for the period 19-30 August. In case you would like to extend your visit to Russia, please, send us special request indicating your preferred period of stay.

Foreign participants and guests of the Conference must possessan entry visa. Visa support will be carried out by the Conference Organizing Committee, as an additional fee component, including:
—  Russian migration service and duty payment (if need, depending on the country of residence),
—  Submission of the original visa invitation to the Russian embassy or consulate in countries of the applicants.

Theadditional fee component does not cover the costs related to visa application process in the Russian Embassy or consulate in countries of applicants.

The Conference Organizing Committee does not guarantee that visa will be granted since this is entirely the prerogative of the Embassy or Consulate of the Russian Federation in the applicant’s country to decide on.

Please note that your passport must be valid no less than 6 months after the trip termination, i.e. at least by February 28, 2017.

Forms for visa request and value of the additional fee component will be available on the Conference web-site.
