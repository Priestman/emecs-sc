FAQ

If you don't find the answer to your question, email us.

I have not received a confirmation e-mail for my profile. What should I do?
•  If you have an email address at the services as google.com, mail.ru, yandex.ru or other public service, please use it for registration. Using national email services could cause problems.
•  Otherwise, please contact us using the support form with the subject "confirm my profile". We will confirm your profile manually.
Does the Conference have any funding/grants available for students from developing countries?
•  No, we do not have this possibility.
I need an official invitation. How can I get it?
•  You can contact us at techsupport with the subject "official invitation" or write to Alexandra Ershova, Technical and Scientific Secretary of the Conference.

Filling in the section "Abstract and paper submission"
•  The amount of added articles is not limited. Every time, wanting to add an abstract, you must fill in this form. Everything that you add will appear under "Added papers". Paper guidelines and the template can be viewed and downloaded here or in a personal account in the upload form;

•  All fields are required. Please note that the number of characters in the abstract is limited to 1500. The bottom of the page counter shows how many characters you can enter.

•  After adding the abstract you will be redirected to the page of your papers.

•  After you receive a confirmation letter that your abstract is accepted, you can add the full text of the paper (format doc or docx). Go to the "added papers" and select from the list. Edit page will have the paragraph, as shown in the figure.
•  At the top of the page all information about your paper is displayed. After you uploaded the full text you will see "File: Uploaded". This means that your paper has been successfully added to our server. You can also delete the paper by clicking on the button at the bottom of this page.

Filling in the section "Coauthors"
•  To add co-authors click on the link in the section "Abstract and paper submission". Select the paper title from the drop-down list to which you want to add a co-author. The filling of the fields are optional.

•  If you want, you can specify a number in the order after the first author.

•  Click "add coauthor". To add another co-author, repeat this procedure. All added coauthors will appear in the same-name list.

•  Clicking on the name of coauthor, you will be redirected to the edit section, where you can change information about or delete.
